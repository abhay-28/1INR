Let Me tell you a Story!!!


Raju was a poor little boy born in Jharkhand who did not go to school. He long wanted to learn and do some things needful for the society. However he was very poor to do this since he could not even afford a one-time meal. His parents were working hard to feed him and his little sister. To financially support his family Raju was doing odd jobs in his village. He wanted to support his father and mother and help make ends meet. In his endeavour to to have a decent meal everyday, one day he met Ramesh.


Ramesh was a well to do guy who wanted to see everybody smile and be happy around. One day he met Raju, At first sight Ramesh understood what Raju was going through and thought of helping him. He told Raju about a campaign that he was running with a local NGO that would help him go to school and get the desired education that he always wanted. “Don’t worry, God and all of us are with you. Tomorrow you shall go to school” were the words Ramesh told Raju.


Hearing this he heaved a sigh of relief and looked upon his ambition to become a Teacher in his village and teach the poor students of his community. With support garnered from his community and the local NGO, He started going to school! Ramesh had indeed enrolled him for getting the basic primary education.
