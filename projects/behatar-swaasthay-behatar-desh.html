Behatar Swaasthay, Behatar Desh

This program focuses on giving early access to nutrition for the needy during the COVID-19 outbreak by serving meals with Roti Bank.

by Pyaribai Girdharilal Ranka Foundation

We at 1INR wish to carry on the tradition of selfless giving- a desire to serve the poor ones during COVID-19. We started off by distributing breakfast, meals, fruits, pulses and more accessible to children in hospital with Roti Bank and Team Hunger of Lions Club of Millennials and Lions Club of Churchgate. With our campaign- Behatar Swaasthay, Behatar Desh we wish to do more to support underprivileged patients facing challenges and their caregivers, beyond our prayers.

 

There are so many families who do not have access to food or afford a one-time meal, which is the basic necessity and leads to poor nutrition to fight the disease. Many underprivileged children in several locations decide to stay hungry to save money. Addressing such issues, 1INR serves free meals, fruits and more to poor children and families of underprivileged patients in the Government Hospital. You can also join our team during the food distribution drive. 

 

We cover a broader aim of addressing hunger needs. Towards this guiding principle is the belief that every home of a poor is served by us. The provider of succor to those who are hungry, because every meal counts! Your donation in this initiative will also go to our second ongoing initiative which is Pehli Roti Dayitva Ki. You don’t have to Cook and Serve but just have to Pay for a Meal to bless the needy people with food.

 

We, at 1INR, with your support in Behatar Swaasthay, Behatar Desh campaign make sure that your support reaches the needy children. Through 1INR, they'll receive proper meals, attention from medical staff and stay hunger-free. But this is only possible with the emergency funds we receive from you for giving them a 2 times meal.

 

We would appreciate a helping hand and making this aim a success. Your small contribution will support Behatar Swaasthay, Behatar Desh with a donation of as low as 1INR and help them sustain their livelihood.
